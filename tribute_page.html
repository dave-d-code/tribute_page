Mr. Mahir Çağri

The Man whom may have started Social Media by Accident..

In the late 1990's, a Turkish man called Mahir Çağri created his own personal HTML page. That's nothing unusual, but in 1998, The Internet wasn't quite as mainstream as it is now. Using his best broken English, Mr Çağri put a picture of himself, and a quote "I kiss you." Such affection, and coupled with his picture caused his website to go viral.
